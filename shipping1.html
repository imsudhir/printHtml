2. Calculate shipping costs for your products

You set up your own product and shipping prices for your customers. When an order is made, you charge your customer and then Printful charges you for the product and shipping to fulfill your customer's order. Whatever is left over after Printful charges you is your profit!

 

The way you set your profit margin is up to you. To help you stay on track with trickier shipping calculations, read the questions we answered below.

How do I calculate shipping costs for orders with one product?

To get the shipping cost for an order with one product, scroll up to our shipping rates table and find the product category with your product. Once you click on it, you'll see listed products that have the same shipping costs, depending on the location you ship to.

 

Take a look at the First product rowâ€”that's the shipping cost for an order containing only one product.

 

Example: The cost of shipping one t-shirt to the USA is $3.99.

How do I calculate shipping costs for orders with similar products?

Similar products are items that are grouped together under one shipping category and have the same shipping rates. For example, hoodies and sweatpants, tote bags and fanny packs are all in one category with the same shipping rate.

To calculate the shipping cost for an order with similar products, scroll up to our shipping rates table and find the category with your products. Once you click on it, you'll see listed products that have the same shipping costs, depending on the location you ship to.

Take a look at the First product rowâ€”that's the shipping cost for an order containing only one product. Then look at the Additional product row and add that price to the one in the First product row. That's it!

Example: The cost for shipping one t-shirt to the USA is $3.99 which is the price in the First product row. Then if an order has 2 t-shirts, the shipping for the second t-shirt will cost $1.25 which is the price in the Additional product row. All together the cost for shipping two t-shirts to the USA is $5.24.

How do I calculate shipping costs for orders with different products?

The products that are not grouped together under a category count as different products in terms of shipping prices. For example, hoodies and all-over print leggings, tote bags and backpacks are in different categories with different pricing.

To calculate the shipping cost for an order with different products, scroll up to our shipping rates table and find both categories with your products. See which of your products has the highest shipping cost in the First product row and use that as a base. Then, to cover the second product, add that product's Additional product price. That's it!

Example: To get the shipping cost for an order with a hoodie and an apron, see whichever one has the highest shipping cost in the First product row. Worldwide shipping for a hoodie is $9.99 and $2 for each additional product, and for an apron, it's $5.99 and $1.25 for each additional product. The highest price for the First product in this example would be the hoodie's, so you should use that and add the apron's Additional product shipping cost to cover it. All together, shipping an apron and a hoodie would cost $11.24 in this example.

How do I calculate shipping costs for orders with products that ship separately?

There are some products that we'll ship separately due to packaging differences. These products are: mugs, framed posters, posters, canvas, stickers, jewelry, bean bags, backpacks, pillows with stuffing, snapback hats.

To calculate the shipping cost for orders with products that ship separately, you'll have to add the shipping price from the First product row of both products.

Example: To get the shipping cost for an order with a hoodie and a mug, add the shipping price from the First product row of both products. Worldwide shipping for a hoodie is $9.99 and $2 for each additional product, and for a mug, it's $8.99 and $4 for each additional product. The shipping cost of an order with a hoodie and a mug would add up to be $18.98 in this example.

If you sell Printful products that require separate shipping, we advise you to set the rates for these products using the costs in the First product row only. This will ensure you don't lose profit on shipping.

How do I calculate shipping costs for non-Printful products that I keep in Printful's warehouse?

To calculate the shipping costs for your non-Printful products, you'll need to check how much our carrier partners would charge for orders containing your products. Find a shipping rate calculator on one of our supported carrier sites and add your product details there. Youâ€™ll see what the approximate shipping rates for this product will be for different shipping destinations.

 

Our shipping algorithm chooses the most cost-effective shipping option for each order. So for you to get the most accurate shipping rates for your warehoused products, we suggest calculating the shipping rates with multiple carriers and using the average.

Get answers to commonly asked questions

Will I get my order in one shipment?

No, not always. We may split an order into multiple shipments for the following reasons:

  • Product packaging. Some of our products are individually packaged to ensure their shape and provide extra cushion and durability. The products we'll always ship separately are mugs, wall art, stickers, jewelry, bean bags, backpacks, pillows with stuffing, and snapback hats.
  • Fulfillment location. In our Product catalog, you'll see that we fulfill products in multiple locations (e.g., US, EU, AU), so if your order consists of products that have different fulfillment locations, we'll ship the products separately.

Shipping costs for orders with multiple shipments will be higher than for orders with a single shipment. See the final shipping costs in the order checkout or read about shipping calculations.

Will I get charged for customs?

If your order's shipped from a facility that's in a different region than your delivery destination, you might have to pay handling and/or customs fees upon delivery. The customs fees may vary depending on the order value, country limits, etc.

To avoid customs fees, order products that we fulfill close to your delivery region. You can sort our products by fulfillment locationsâ€”in the Product catalog, open the product category you're interested in (like custom embroidered hats) and, in the left side menu under Fulfilled in, select the fulfillment location closest to you.

Can I return/exchange my ordered products?

If you havenâ€™t received your order or notice an issue with the products, please reach out to our customer support team and weâ€™ll help you get it solved. Please read our Return Policy for more details.

Do shipping prices change based on product fulfillment locations?

No, the shipping rates are not impacted by the location where the products are fulfilled. For example, it'll cost the same to ship a t-shirt from the US to Germany as it would be to ship the same t-shirt from the EU.
