What is print-on-demand?

Print-on-demand (or POD) is an order fulfillment method where items are printed as soon as an order is made, often without order minimums.

What does drop shipping mean?

The term POD is often combined with drop shipping to describe a service where items are printed to order and shipped directly from the manufacturer to the buyer on behalf of the customer selling the goods.

How is it different from wholesale or ordering in bulk?

Retail businesses normally make money buying products in bulk at wholesale prices and selling them at higher prices in smaller volumes. But there's a good reason print-on-demand fulfillment is becoming more and more popular.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if you don't have a product I'm looking for?

You can always submit a feature request! If you want to start selling ASAP, you can outsource the product from another supplier and fulfill orders using our Warehousing & Fulfillment service.

Is there any Printful branding on the packaging?

No—we're completely white label. The order will have your branding on it, so it'll look like it came from you.

How long does fulfillment take?

2-7 business days, depending on the product type and availability.

Where do you get your products from?

We work with the best suppliers in the business—Bella+Canvas, Gildan, and more. Here's more info on our partners.

When do I pay for fulfillment?

When your customer buys an item fulfilled by Printful on your storefront, two transactions will take place. Your customer will pay you, and we'll charge you when the order comes through. You'll need to set up a billing method on your Printful account for that.

Where do returns go?

For North America, the default returns address is our Charlotte facility. For Europe, it's our Riga facility. If you'd like to handle returned items yourself, you can change it to any other address.

Where do you ship from?

Depending on the product availability for each order and the end-customer's address, we ship from either Los Angeles, Charlotte, Tijuana, or Riga.

Where do you ship to?

We ship worldwide; however, there are a few countries we don't ship to.

How can I check the quality of my products?

We always recommend ordering samples to make sure you're happy with what you're selling. Sample orders are 20% off with free shipping.

What if my customer enters the wrong address?

If the local post office can figure out the correct one, they'll take care of the order. If not, the order will be sent to the return address and you'll have to reship it.
