Can I store any product I want at your warehouse?

You can keep any product at our warehouses as long as it meets the following storing requirements:

  1. Has dimensions smaller than 15 × 15 × 10 in. (38 × 38 × 25 cm)
  2. Isn't worth more than $750 (700€) a piece
  3. Isn't perishable
  4. Follows our Acceptable Content Guidelines and the Warehousing & Fulfillment Terms of Service

Where are your warehouses located?

We currently offer warehousing services at our fulfillment centers in the US—Charlotte, North Carolina, and Los Angeles, California, in Canada—Toronto, Ontario, and at our Europe locations in Riga, Latvia, and Barcelona, Spain.
